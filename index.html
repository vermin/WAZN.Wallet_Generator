Seed Phrase

Copy your Seed Phrase and Private Keys. If you lose access to your wallet, these keys can be used to restore your WAZN wallet at any time. Keep this information safe and private.



Restoration with QR code is currently not available. Please enter your seed above and click 'Restore'.

Windows: wazn-wallet-cli.exe --generate-from-keys walletname.wazn
Linux: ./wazn-wallet-cli --generate-from-keys walletname.wazn
